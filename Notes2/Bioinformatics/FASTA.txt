=== Special Additional Letters: ===
B	Asx		Aspartic acid (D) or Asparagine (N) (Cannot be determined unambiguously)
Z	Glx		Glutamic acid (E) or Glutamine (Q) (Cannot be determined unambiguously)
J	Xle		Leucine (L) or Isoleucine (I) (Less common, but used to indicate ambiguity)
X	Any		Any amino acid (The position is occupied, but the identity is unknown)
* Stop	Termination codon (Indicates the end of a translated sequence)
U	Sec		Selenocysteine (A 21st, rare amino acid)
O	Pyl		Pyrrolysine (A 22nd, rare amino acid)
