====== Environments ======
Created Thursday 17 July 2025

**List all environments:** 
conda env list

**Show all environment locations:**
conda config --show envs_dirs

=== Example: Create new environment with RDKit: ===
conda create -n envname python=3.9 rdkit -c conda-forge

=== Rename Environment: ===
conda rename -n old_name  new_name 

=== Remove Environment: ===
conda remove -n ENV_NAME --all
