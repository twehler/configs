====== BasicCommands ======
Created Sunday 02 November 2025

=== Copy files: ===
cp [[/target]] [[/destination]]

=== Copy directories: ===
cp -r [[/target]] [[/destination]]

=== Remove (same syntax as cp): ===
rm

=== Move: ===
mv

=== create symlinks: ===
sudo ln -s [[/folder]] [[/link/destination]]

=== change screen color to yellowish (omit most blue lights): ===
xsct 3000 (lower if needed)
