====== Disks ======
Created Sunday 19 October 2025

=== View all  mounted disks ===
df -h

=== View all plugged Disks and their UUID: ===
lsblk -f

=== File system check: ===



=== Read-Only test: ===
sudo umount [[/dev/sdXx]] **(very important, otherwise risk of corruption!)**
sudo badblocks -sv [[/dev/sdXx]]

=== Deep health check ===
sudo smartctl -t long /dev/sdXx
